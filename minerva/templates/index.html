GreenUp Weekly Challenge

Convince 7 people to install 1 LED lightbulb in their home.

Why? Installing a single LED lightbulb might reduce your energy consumption significantly, saving you as much as £10 per year as a nice bonus. When bought on usual terms, it will become worth the price in 18 months, while its lifespan is more than 20 years.

Nonetheless, people still are prejudiced against LEDs, deeming them expensive and "cold". By getting them to actually try one out, you can free people from old misconceptions.

How? It is up to you, but here are few suggestions. You can approach them directly and test how convincing you might be. However, we have secured you a deal through Phillips: enter a voucher PH1L3D43 on their e-shop to get one for free.

Another option is to use this FirstCall Home assist scheme that allows you to swap up to 20 old lightbulbs for brand new LEDs for free. It's sponsored by Scottish Government, so there is no catch to it.

Ultimately, be creative! It is a really tough task, but hey, that's why it is rewarding. Challenge your friends to do the same, it's much more fun and effective this way!
